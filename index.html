Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need controllers?

Nope! Just one copy of the game on a PC or TV, and everyone joins using their own smartphone.

How many players?

Couch Cup supports 3 to 12 players locally or online. The more the merrier!

Is it online?

Yes! You can play with friends in the same room or stream it over Discord/Zoom for remote play.

Is there a drinking mode?

You bet. Toggle it on for spicy prompts and challenges designed for adult audiences.

Each player can personalize their avatar for any game using our completely optional account system.
