Privacy Policy for Couch Cup

Effective Date: 11/9/2024

Thank you for choosing Couch Cup! Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Couch Cup (referred to as "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our game application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve the App:

1.1. Information You Provide

  • User Data: When you create an account, you may provide a username, email address, and password.
  • Avatar Customization: If you choose to customize your avatar, we may store the customization choices you make.
  • User-Generated Content: Information such as text or images you submit in response to prompts or games within the App.

1.2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the App, such as gameplay statistics, session duration, and in-app purchases.
  • Device Information: We may collect data such as your device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers to optimize the App for your device.
  • Log Data: Our servers may log IP addresses, access times, app crashes, and other related data.

1.3. Third-Party Information

If you choose to link your account with a third-party provider (such as social media), we may collect information from that provider to streamline the login process and enhance your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App, including processing your gameplay data.
  • Personalize your experience and customize the App's content.
  • Communicate with you regarding support, updates, and new features.
  • Improve, test, and monitor the effectiveness of our App.
  • Conduct research, analysis, and reporting to make the App better.

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with third parties in limited circumstances, including:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who provide services such as cloud storage, analytics, and customer support.
  • Legal Compliance and Safety: If required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of users, we may disclose information.
  • Aggregated Data: We may share aggregated, anonymized information that does not identify you personally with our partners for research and analysis purposes.

4. Your Choices and Controls

You have control over your data:

  • Account Information: You may update or delete your account information by accessing your profile settings within the App.
  • Data Collection: You can adjust your device settings to limit the data we collect. Disabling certain permissions may affect the App's functionality.
  • Marketing Communications: If you receive marketing communications from us, you can opt-out by following the unsubscribe instructions or updating your preferences in the App.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data, although we may retain some aggregated or anonymized data for analytics.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no internet-based application is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Couch Cup is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after such changes indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
